Before you order

Make sure you know what devices you need, as amending or cancelling an order will cause delays.

It may help to:

  • check device specifications
  • give someone else access so they can place orders

How to order

Orders are fullfilled by our technology partner TechSource. Select 'Start now' to go to their website.

If you try to order more than 10 routers and 286 devices, the order will be cancelled.

For large orders (more than 150 devices), we'll call your delivery contact to arrange delivery.

Most devices will be delivered within 5 working days of an order being confirmed, subject to stock availability.

Combining allocations into one order

You can combine allocations for all your schools and colleges, and have all devices delivered to one location. If you want devices delivered directly to different locations, place a seperate order for each delivery address.

The TechSource website will ask you for a unique reference number (URN). You can use a URN from any of the schools or colleges that you manage.
