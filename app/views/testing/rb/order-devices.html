Before you order

Make sure you’re certain of what devices you need, as amending or cancelling an order will cause delays.

It may help to:

  • check device specifications
  • give someone else access so they can place orders

If you order iPads, we’ll ask for your Apple School Manager (ASM). Go to the Apple School website to find your ASM, or to create an account.

Operation Warm Welcome

There’s a different process for ordering devices for children arriving under Operation Warm Welcome.

You can order laptops and routers by filling in this form.

Find out more about Operation Warm Welcome and what devices are available.

How to order

You need to place orders on a website called TechSource.

If you try to order more than your allocation the order will be cancelled.

Most devices will be delivered within 5 working days of an order being confirmed, subject to stock availability.

Combining allocations into one order

You can combine allocations for all your schools and colleges, and have all devices delivered to one location. If you want devices delivered directly to different locations, place a separate order for each delivery address.

When using TechSource, you will be asked for a URN. Use any URN for one of the schools or colleges you manage.
